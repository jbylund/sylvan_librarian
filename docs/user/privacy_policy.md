# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## 1. Introduction

Arcane Tutor is committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Magic: The Gathering card search engine.

**Related Documentation:**

- [terms_of_service.md](../user/terms_of_service.md) - User agreement and service terms
- [legal.md](../legal/legal.md) - Data sources and legal compliance
- [about.md](../user/about.md) - Project information

**Key Points:**

- We do not require user accounts
- We do not collect or store personal information
- We do not sell or share any data
- We use minimal browser storage for preferences only

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do **NOT** collect, store, or process:

- Personal identification information (names, email addresses, etc.)
- Payment information (we don't sell anything)
- User account data (we don't have user accounts)
- Location data beyond what your browser automatically sends
- Browsing history or activity tracking
- Cookies for tracking or advertising purposes

## 3. Information We Do Collect

### 3.1 Browser Local Storage

We use your browser's `localStorage` feature to store **only**:

- **Theme preference**: Whether you prefer light or dark mode

This information:

- Is stored locally on your device only
- Is never transmitted to our servers
- Can be cleared by clearing your browser's local storage
- Does not identify you personally

### 3.2 Standard Server Logs

Our web servers may automatically log standard technical information:

- IP addresses
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring URLs
- Access times and dates
- Pages requested

This information:

- Is used only for technical maintenance and security
- Is not linked to personally identifiable information
- May be retained for a limited time for operational purposes
- Is never sold or shared with third parties

### 3.3 Error Monitoring (If Enabled)

If error monitoring is enabled, we may collect:

- Error messages and stack traces
- Browser and device information
- Page URLs where errors occurred

This data is used solely to identify and fix technical issues.

## 4. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used only to:

- Provide and maintain the Service
- Remember your theme preference
- Monitor and improve Service performance
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Ensure Service security and prevent abuse

We do **NOT** use information for:

- Marketing or advertising
- Profiling or behavioral tracking
- Selling or sharing with third parties
- Any purpose beyond Service operation

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Data Sources

We retrieve card data from **Scryfall's bulk data API**.
When you use our Service:

- Your searches are processed on our servers
- We do not send your queries directly to Scryfall
- We comply with Scryfall's API Terms of Service

### 5.2 External Links

The Service contains links to external sites:

- [Scryfall](https://scryfall.com) - Card data provider
- [Wizards of the Coast](https://company.wizards.com) - Magic: The Gathering owner
- [GitHub](https://github.com/jbylund/arcane_tutor) - Source code repository

These sites have their own privacy policies.
We are not responsible for their practices.

### 5.3 CDN and Font Services

We may use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to serve:

- Card images
- Font files (Mana symbols, Beleren, MPlantin)

These services may log access information according to their own privacy policies.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the Service, but:

- No internet transmission is 100% secure
- We cannot guarantee absolute security
- You use the Service at your own risk

Since we don't collect personal information, there is minimal risk of data breach affecting users.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
The Service is not directed at children, and we do not market to children.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Theme Preference

You can:

- Clear your theme preference by clearing browser local storage
- Toggle between light and dark themes using the theme button

### 8.2 Do Not Track

We do not track users, so "Do Not Track" browser settings do not affect the Service.

### 8.3 Data Access and Deletion

Since we don't collect or store personal information, there is no data to access or delete.
If you have concerns, you can:

- Clear your browser's local storage
- Clear your browser cache
- Stop using the Service

## 9. International Users

The Service is hosted in the United States.
If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your connection may be routed through international servers.
We do not intentionally collect personal information from users in any jurisdiction.
